\textbf{Motivation}: According to the World Economic Forum, the construction
industry currently accounts for about $6\%$ of the world gross domestic product
(GDP) and is expected to reach around $14.7\%$ in $2030$. Analogous to the
Industry 4.0, breakthrough technologies have been adapted for construction
applications; this transformation has been called as Construction 4.0.
RoBétArmé aims toward a step-change in Construction 4.0 by automating
particularly laborious construction tasks in all phases of shotcrete
application. To this end RoBétArmé will deliver collaborative construction
mobile manipulators, consisting of (a) Inspection Reconnaissance robot (IRR) to
address fast, high precision modeling and rebar reinforcement through metal
additive manufacturing and (b) a Shotcrete and Finishing mobile robot (SFR) to
address autonomous shotcrete application and surface finishing.
