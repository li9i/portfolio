\chapter{Summary}

This portfolio contains the gist and some details on a number of projects
which I was a member or the sole executor of. By no means is it a complete
volume of my engagements or the things I have performed or learned or have
ground away at.

Up to now I have had the pleasure of working in three research projects: one
EU-funded (RoBetArme) and two state-funded (RELIEF and CULTUREID). In all three
I worked as a robotics and control software engineer. My path passed through
\texttt{ROS} and \texttt{ROS 2}, \texttt{C++}, \texttt{Python}, shell
scripting, \texttt{Docker}, and \texttt{git}. The first part of this volume is
dedicated to these three projects. The rest of the projects you will see, with
the exception of my stint at PANDORA, Aristotle University's robotics team
taking part in RoboCup,---with this exception all others are MSc-level
theses and projects undertaken while I was a student at KTH. I have not
included components of my doctorate thesis as I would talk your ears off for it.

All in all I have worked on robotics, control, state estimation, computer
vision, and machine learning projects. I have been the architect of code,
groups of repositories, integration, documentation, academic papers, and other
systems, as well as their developer, writer, and deployer.
